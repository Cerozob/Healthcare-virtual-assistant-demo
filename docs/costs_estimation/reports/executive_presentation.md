## Slide 9: Amazon Nova Micro Cost Analysis

### Revolutionary Cost Optimization Opportunity

**Amazon Nova Micro** represents a significant cost optimization opportunity for the AWSomeBuilder2 healthcare management system. As Amazon's newest text-only foundation model, it delivers the lowest latency responses in the Nova family at a very low cost.

### Cost Comparison Analysis

| Model | Monthly Cost | Cost per Interaction | Savings vs Claude 3.5 Haiku |
|-------|--------------|---------------------|------------------------------|
| **Claude 3.5 Haiku** | $11,940 | $0.597 | Baseline |
| **Claude Haiku 4.5** | $14,604 | $0.730 | -$2,664 (22% increase) |
| **Amazon Nova Micro** | $2,400-$4,800 | $0.120-$0.240 | $7,140-$9,540 (60-80% savings) |

### Nova Micro Advantages

#### Cost Benefits

- **60-80% Cost Reduction**: Potential savings of $7,140-$9,540/month
- **Lowest Latency**: Fastest response times in Nova family
- **128K Context Length**: Sufficient for most healthcare interactions
- **Customization Support**: Fine-tuning and model distillation available

#### Healthcare Use Case Alignment

- **Text Summarization**: Medical record summaries
- **Content Classification**: Symptom categorization and triage
- **Interactive Chat**: Patient Q&A and basic consultations
- **Simple Reasoning**: Basic medical calculations and recommendations

### Implementation Strategy

#### Hybrid Model Approach

1. **Primary Model**: Amazon Nova Micro (90-95% of interactions)
   - Simple queries, summaries, classifications
   - Basic patient interactions
   - Standard medical Q&A

2. **Secondary Model**: Claude 3.5 Haiku (5-10% of interactions)
   - Complex medical reasoning
   - Multi-step diagnostic workflows
   - Advanced document analysis

#### Expected Cost Structure with Nova Micro

| Component | Current Cost | With Nova Micro | Savings |
|-----------|--------------|-----------------|---------|
| **Foundation Models** | $11,940 | $3,600 | $8,340 |
| **Total AI Stack** | $18,580 | $10,240 | $8,340 |
| **Total System** | $28,440 | $20,100 | $8,340 |

### Risk Assessment and Mitigation

#### Potential Risks

- **Performance Gap**: Nova Micro may have lower accuracy for complex queries
- **Feature Limitations**: Text-only model lacks multimodal capabilities
- **Integration Complexity**: Requires intelligent routing between models

#### Mitigation Strategies

- **Gradual Migration**: Start with 50% Nova Micro, increase based on performance
- **Quality Monitoring**: Real-time accuracy tracking and automatic fallback
- **A/B Testing**: Compare user satisfaction between models
- **Hybrid Routing**: Intelligent query classification for optimal model selection

### Financial Impact

#### Monthly Savings Breakdown

- **Direct Model Costs**: $8,340/month savings
- **Reduced Guardrails Processing**: $2,000/month (fewer complex interactions)
- **Lower Infrastructure Costs**: $500/month (reduced compute requirements)
- **Total Monthly Savings**: $10,840/month

#### Annual Financial Impact

- **Year 1 Savings**: $130,080
- **3-Year Savings**: $390,240
- **ROI on Implementation**: 2,500% (implementation cost ~$15,000)

### Implementation Timeline

#### Phase 1: Pilot (Month 1)

- Deploy Nova Micro for 25% of simple interactions
- Implement quality monitoring and fallback mechanisms
- Validate cost savings and performance metrics

#### Phase 2: Scale (Month 2-3)

- Increase Nova Micro usage to 70% of interactions
