# Product Overview

AWSomeBuilder 2 is an AI-powered healthcare solution designed for AnyCompany to modernize patient data management and medical workflow automation.

## Core Purpose

- **Conversational Interface**: AI chatbot for medics to record patient symptoms and notes
- **Automated Scheduling**: Agentic AI system that automatically schedules medical exams and treatments based on patient symptoms and company protocols
- **Data Integration**: Connects to internal patient databases, diagnosis records, and exam scheduling systems

## Target Users

- Healthcare professionals (doctors, nurses, medical staff)
- Healthcare administrators managing patient workflows
- IT teams deploying and maintaining the system

## Key Requirements

- **Compliance**: Must meet healthcare security and privacy regulations (HIPAA-like requirements)
- **Scalability**: Architecture must scale to meet varying medical staff demands without resource waste
- **Integration**: Seamless connection to existing company data sources and medical systems
- **Automation**: Reduce manual, error-prone processes in patient care scheduling

## Business Value

Transforms manual, error-prone patient data entry and exam scheduling into an automated, AI-driven workflow that improves efficiency and reduces medical errors.
